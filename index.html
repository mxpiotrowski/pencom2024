Inform 7 games created by the students in the course Introduction to Computational Thinking (Prof. Dr.-Ing. Michael Piotrowski) at the University of Lausanne, spring semester 2024.

  • Mila Frey, Jeanne Robert, Carlo Rossel, and Nora Thaçi: Archipelago
  • Mirlinda Sinanaj and Timothée Rochat: Escape from the Forbidden Island
  • Tonia Ramogida and Roxane Borgeaud: AperoQuest
  • Sabrine Specker and Annaël Prevost: Mystères au cinéma
  • Jonathan Marquez, Cinthia Lopes Henriques, and Arthur Garbi: Ouroboros
  • Léa Donnet, Valentine Cuenot, Dylan Perlasca, and Gianmarco Sapienza: theProtestQuest
